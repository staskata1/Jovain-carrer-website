About SPGE "John Atanasoff"

SPGE "John Atanasoff" is one of the leading technical schools in Bulgaria, named after the famous inventor and co-creator of the first electronic digital computer. The school is known for its excellent education in fields such as programming, electronics, and IT, preparing students for future careers in technology and engineering.

With modern laboratories, experienced teachers, and partnerships with leading companies, SPGE "John Atanasoff" provides a high-quality education environment for its students. It has a rich tradition of success in national and international competitions, ensuring students receive the best preparation for their future careers.
